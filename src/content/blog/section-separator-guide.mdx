## üìù Best Practices

### 1. Consistent Styling

Choose 1-2 ribbon styles for your entire blog:

```jsx
// ‚úÖ Good - Consistent
<SectionSeparator style="ribbon" />
<SectionSeparator style="ribbon" color="blue" />
<SectionSeparator style="ribbon" color="purple" />

// ‚ùå Inconsistent
<SectionSeparator style="ribbon" />
<SectionSeparator style="tag" />
<SectionSeparator style="zigzag" />
```

### 2. Color Meaning

Assign semantic meaning to colors:

- **Blue** ‚Üí Information, tips
- **Yellow** ‚Üí Warnings, cautions
- **Red** ‚Üí Errors, important alerts
- **Green** ‚Üí Success, go-ahead
- **Purple** ‚Üí Premium, advanced topics
- **Orange** ‚Üí Updates, changes

### 3. Position Guidelines

- **Left** ‚Üí Best for blog posts (natural reading)
- **Center** ‚Üí Good for formal documentation
- **Right** ‚Üí Creative layouts, unique designs

### 5. Height Selection

- **thin** ‚Üí Suitable for lengthy ribbon without title or for section completion
- **normal** ‚Üí Standard/default width
- **thick** ‚Üí Important subsections
